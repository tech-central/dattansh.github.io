About Us

Data Nova: Transcend through data

The amount of data that is currently collected and processed is perhaps the most spectacular result of the digital revolution. There has never been such an information explosion in human history. Smartphones, sensors, and connected vehicles and appliances, among other digital artefacts, are the reason behind this data explosion. Much of what we touch and work with now creates data automatically. The data boom over the last ten years has generated a virtuous cycle of data analysis and action, resulting in new insights. As firms scramble to modernise their businesses and make data-driven decisions, we've seen them acquire more data than ever before.

Data Nova will take you to a virtual world where data and innovation converge to create an explosion of problem-solving, analytics and excitement.
