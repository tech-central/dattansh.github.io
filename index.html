About Us

Data Nova: Transcend through data





The amount of data that is currently collected and processed is perhaps the most spectacular result of the digital revolution. There has never been such an information explosion in human history. Smartphones, sensors, and connected vehicles and appliances, among other digital artefacts, are the reason behind this data explosion. Much of what we touch and work with now creates data automatically.

The data boom over the last ten years has generated a virtuous cycle of data analysis and action, resulting in new insights. As firms scramble to modernise their businesses and make data-driven decisions, we've seen them acquire more data than ever before.

Data Nova will take you to a virtual world where data and innovation converge to create an explosion of problem-solving, analytics and excitement.

Come and join us in this journey to the space of data and experience extravagance in terms of knowledge sharing, and competitions.

Code Hunters

All you need to know about Code Hunters
Codehunters is a coding contest in which the participants get to showcase their problem solving skills via coding. A set of incomplete codes will be put forward in a cryptic manner to the participants which need to be solved sequentially to yield the answers. Generating the correct answers in every step is essential for the participant to make the next move and finish the complete code.
